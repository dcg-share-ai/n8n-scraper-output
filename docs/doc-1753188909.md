# Submit a Complaint or Comment to BoB Finance

BoB Finance greatly values customer feedback, including concerns, recommendations, suggestions, and complaints, to continuously improve service standards and ensure maximum customer satisfaction.

## How to Submit a Complaint or Comment

You can submit your feedback directly via the online form provided on their website.

### Required Information for Submission:

When filling out the form, please provide the following details:

*   **Type of Request:** Select either 'Complaint' or 'Comments'.
*   **Customer Name**
*   **Customer Address**
*   **Phone Number**
*   **P.O.Box Number**
*   **Email Address**
*   **Name of the Staff Dealt With:** The name of the staff member with whom the issue was discussed or the complaint was raised.
*   **Previous Communication:** Indicate if the issue has been previously communicated (Yes/No).
*   **Preferred Contact Method:** Choose Phone, Mail, or Email.
*   **Preferred Contact Time**
*   **Product/Service** (related to your feedback)
*   **Type of Complaint** (if applicable)
*   **Complaint Details:** Provide a detailed description of your complaint or comment.
*   **Security Code:** Enter the displayed security code.
